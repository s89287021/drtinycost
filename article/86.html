Some time later.

Indeed, the older one gets, the more mischievous they become. Just before leaving, Sun DaFu couldn't resist giving Pu Lianhua a squeeze on her buttocks.

"Uncle DaFu, still as vigorous and full of vigor as ever!"

Sun DaFu walked away with a satisfied look, laughing sinisterly, when he suddenly heard a voice beside him, startling him.

Turning his head, he saw it was Zhu Tianlei.

"What are you talking nonsense about?"

"You're talking nonsense? Uncle DaFu just had such an intense encounter with Aunt Lianhua, and you've already forgotten so quickly?"

Zhu Tianlei laughed heartily, but Sun DaFu's face darkened.

"W-what did you see?"

Sun DaFu still clung to a shred of hope. After all, he had been intimate with Pu Lianhua for two years without anyone ever catching wind of it.

"I saw everything that should and shouldn't be seen, heard everything that should and shouldn't be heard. The only question is what Aunt DaFu would think if she knew about this."

Upon hearing Zhu Tianlei mention his wife, Sun DaFu immediately panicked.

He was a son-in-law who had married into the family, owning neither the house nor the land. Furthermore, his wife was known throughout the village as a formidable woman. If she found out, he would be in big trouble.

"Cousin, look, we're neighbors living here together. As your senior uncle, please pretend you didn't see anything, okay?"

Sun DaFu knew for sure that Zhu Tianlei had seen everything. He couldn't afford to take the risk, so he could only grovel and plead.

"Neighbors? Uncle DaFu, when I was thrown on the mountain by my father and came back alone, did you think about neighbors then? Or when rumors spread that my father's leg injury was due to showing off, did you ever mention these four words?"

Sun Dafu's face turned pale. This matter had already passed more than a decade, and he truly hadn't expected to end up in Zhu Tianlei's hands one day. If he had known this would happen, he definitely would have been more restrained back then.

"Big nephew, it was Uncle Daful who was wrong back then. Big nephew, don't be like your uncle, okay?"

Sun Dafu really did know how to bend and stretch. However, Zhu Tianlei harbored old grievances and had new demands; he couldn't just let the matter pass with a few kind words from the other party.

"What Uncle Daful is saying isn’t right at all. I'm the junior, how could I dare to compare myself with Uncle Daful? Rest assured, I definitely won't spread rumors like Uncle Daful did. I will definitely tell Auntie Daful the truth about this matter."

Zhu Tianlei finished speaking and turned to leave. Sun Dafu was so scared his legs went weak.

"Big nephew, big nephew, don’t rush off! We can still discuss things. Look, what can I do to let you go this time?"

A sneer flashed across Zhu Tianlei's mind, but he remained expressionless on the outside.

"What Uncle Daful is saying, I don't understand. I just find your actions distasteful. What’s there to negotiate about?"

"That's not right, big nephew. Look, how about if I give you two thousand yuan, then you can pretend you didn’t see this happen, okay?"

Sun Dafu said in pain.

"Uncle Daful, Zhu Tianlei isn't short of that amount of money."

"Wait a minute, 5000, 5000 yuan, okay?"

Sun Dafu was truly desperate. If his wife found out about this matter, it wouldn’t just be a matter of 5000 yuan. Moreover, as long as he could keep Zhu Tianlei at bay for now, he would find a way to get the money back later.

"Uncle Daful, are you really willing to negotiate with me?"

"Yes yes yes, of course I am."

Seeing that Zhu Tianlei was somewhat wavering, Sun Dafu quickly responded.

"That's fine, I want the eight mu of land on Uncle Liang Gangzi's property."

"No!"

Sun Dafu refused immediately without thinking; that land was for his retirement and could not be given away.

"Let's talk about this at your uncle's house later!"

"You… wait!"

Seeing Zhu Tianlei turn to leave, Sun Dafu panicked again.

"Other than that, there's nothing else to discuss."

Zhu Tianlei's attitude was firm, his gaze unusually cold. After an intense struggle in Sun Dafu's heart, he gritted his teeth and nodded.

"Alright, but I can't make this decision on my own; I need to go home and talk to your aunt."

"Fine! I'll wait for Uncle Dafu's message!"

Sun Dafu scratched his head.

"Cousin, you…"

"Don't worry, Uncle Dafu. I only want the land; I didn't see anything else."

With that said, Zhu Tianlei turned and left. This time, Sun Dafu did not speak, watching as Zhu Tianlei's figure disappeared at the turn of the meadow.

"Sigh, damn it!"

Muttering under his breath, Sun Dafu limped out of the meadow.

With the earlier incident, Zhu Tianlei was in a good mood, whistling as he walked briskly. Sun Dafu's eight-tenths of an acre had been secured. He still needed to carefully consider the remaining one and two-tenths of an acre. After much thought, it seemed that Yao Gang would have to help with this matter.

Zhu Tianlei first went to the village office, but Yao Gang was not there. Zhu then went to Yao Gang's house, where he was greeted by Yao Gang's son, Yao Jianxiong.

The nineteen-year-old Yao Jianxiong looked almost identical to his father, a younger version of Yao Gang, just slightly taller. Seeing Zhu Tianlei, Yao Jianxiong appeared very happy.
