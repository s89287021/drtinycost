"Big Brother... you hit me?"

"Apologize!"

Lu Anti's face darkened to an even deeper shade.

Zhu Tianlei, on the other hand, remained motionless, seemingly enjoying the commotion before him.

"Brother, it was my mistake. I was short-sighted not to recognize true talent. Brother, don't hold me in low regard!"

After a long hesitation, Jin Ya finally opened his mouth and apologized. He understood that a small loss could prevent bigger problems.

"Yes, Brother, there really was a misunderstanding today. Why don't you let it go? Or how about I treat you to some fun at Jinhantidi?"

Lu Kang blushed, acknowledging his inferior skill and accepting defeat.

"Forget it! Lu Kang, what you do is under the watch of heaven. Always stay cautious when walking by the riverside; I hope we don't have another chance to meet!"

After watching the commotion, Zhu Tianlei casually tossed out this remark, causing Lu Kang's face to pale, but he didn't dare to disagree.

"Yes yes yes, Brother says so!"

Including Golden Teeth and several other Black Eagle Gang brothers were quite puzzled. The person who was nodding submissively and fawning before them was their big brother?

Zhu Tianlei finished speaking without another word, opened the car door, and got in.

In the driver's seat, Hao Xinmei gave Zhu Tianlei a thumbs up and then stepped on the accelerator, sending the car roaring away.

This left several bewildered Black Eagle Gang henchmen looking at each other.

Hao Xinmei drove directly to the hotel where Zhu Tianlei was staying. Yao Gang had already left, but he had left a note for Zhu Tianlei at the front desk.

"Alright, now I'm all alone!"

Zhu Tianlei waved the note in front of Hao Xinmei's face with a pitiful look.

"Don't think you know what plans are in my mind; it won't work!"

Zhu Tianlei approached meekly.

"What plans are in your mind?"

"You... hmph!"

Hao Xinmei blushed again. Zhu Tianlei was completely different when saving lives compared to his usual self.

"Haha, just kidding! You really got angry, huh? I'll stay at the hotel for one more night and leave early tomorrow. It's been a few days since I came out!"

Seeing Hao Xinmei's rosy red face, Zhu Tianlei grinned several times. He loved seeing the woman in front of him pouting with a huff.

"I'll come pick you up tomorrow morning!"

Watching Hao Xinmei leave, Zhu Tianlei indulged in his fantasies again before returning to his room.

Since he had slept for a long time during the day, he wasn't very tired at that moment. Therefore, he plugged in his phone charger and sat on the bed, closing his eyes to meditate calmly.

The ball of spiritual energy in his dantian was now as full as a fist and could change size according to how much spiritual energy he used.

Every time he used spiritual energy to treat someone, it seemed to deplete first but then doubled the depleted amount, becoming even more stable and pure.

For instance, within half an hour of absorbing it, his meridians and qi flow were incredibly smooth.

An hour later, Zhu Tianlei ended his meditation without any fatigue; instead, he felt refreshed and clear-minded. His gaze was sharp as a lightning bolt, piercing through the window to view the city of Yunhai's myriad lights and colorful neon signs.

After taking a quick shower, Zhu Tianlei turned on the TV and pulled out his phone, which had already charged to eighty percent.

As soon as he turned it on, the phone vibrated incessantly, startling him.

There were seventeen missed calls, over sixty WeChat messages, and two promotional text messages.

Lying in bed, Zhu Tianlei first glanced at the missed calls. Among them were several from Hao Xinmei, Min Shengjun, Ma Lanlan, and Yao Gang, but there was also an unknown number with four missed calls.

After some thought, Zhu Tianlei pressed the redial button.

"Hello, who is this?"

.......

"Hello? Speak up! Who are you?"

......

Zhu Tianlei repeated himself several times, but still received no response from the other end of the line, which made him increasingly frustrated.

What a mystery!

He browsed through WeChat and his Moments, then checked a few articles about herb procurement and cultivation. Soon, he felt drowsy and fell asleep in nothing but his underwear.

The next morning.
