"Auntie Liu?"

"Let Tianlei go instead; his medical skills are high, he can even bring the dead back to life!"

Ma Lanlan’s face turned pale, feeling extremely embarrassed.

So Auntie Liu didn’t trust her medical skills. She recalled being the only doctor in Hagemao Village before, and everyone from inside and outside the village would smile and call her Ma Doctor whenever they saw her. But now, she had lost her standing in the hearts of the villagers, far behind Zhu Tianlei.

Even Auntie Liu, who was usually close to her, thought so.

"Auntie Liu, without Lanlan’s help, my medical skills are at least halved. I can only feel confident in diagnosing when Lanlan goes with me."

Zhu Tianlei stood up, his expression rather stern.

Liu Shen’er didn’t understand; could medical skills be tied to someone?

But from what Zhu Tianlei said, he wanted her to go with him, no matter the reason.

"Lanzi, Auntie Liu is begging you! Please go check on him quickly. If we delay any longer, I’m afraid...”

"Alright, Aunty Liu, let's go quickly!"

So, Aunty Liu led the way, followed by Zhu Tianlei and Ma Lanlan, as they hurried towards the western end of the village where Aunty Liu lived.

The moment they entered the courtyard, Zhu Tianlei inexplicably felt a cold aura emanating faintly from the house.

He knew that the cold aura was one form of illness or evil energy, just like the spirit energy within his body. However, while spirit energy healed, ill and evil energies did the opposite.

Upon entering the room, they saw a woman in her thirties sprawled on the kang (a traditional Chinese bed), weeping uncontrollably. Two men, one old and one young, stood by the edge of the kang with despondent faces, creating an oppressive atmosphere throughout the room.

Zhu Tianlei glanced at the kang and noticed a little boy lying beside it, his complexion tinged with purple and cheeks swollen. His lips were almost black, making him look like he had been poisoned at first glance.

"Mom, Huizi is not going to make it... wuwu..."

Seeing Aunty Liu enter, the woman who was weeping uncontrollably on the kang stood up. Her voice was hoarse and her eyes swollen beyond recognition.

"Don't say such nonsense; my grandson's life is strong!"

Aunty Liu frowned at her daughter-in-law but tears were already streaming down her face as she turned to Zhu Tianlei.

"Tianlei, Aunty, please help me... if anything happens to Huizi, this family will fall apart... Tianlei..."

Suddenly, Aunty Liu fell to her knees before Zhu Tianlei. The daughter-in-law standing beside them followed suit, causing Ma Lanlan's eyes to well up with tears.

"Aunty Liu, Sister-in-Law, please get up; I will do my best to save Huizi!"
