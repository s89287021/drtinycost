The Xiaohei Stream mentioned by Zhang Yuntao is a tributary of the Daling River. It flows through Heihulin and forms a small stream in a ravine below Hulu Cave. Because the rocks in the ravine are deep brown or black, the stream looks black, hence its name, Xiaohei Stream.

Xiaohei Stream is not far from where they were standing. Along the way, Zhu Tianlei held Zhang Yuntao's hand, and the two of them seemed like newly-in-love lovers, chatting and laughing, exchanging glances filled with affection.

Soon, they heard a gurgling sound of running water near their ears.

"Eh?"

"What's wrong, Tianlei?"

Seeing Zhu Tianlei raise his eyebrows in surprise, Zhang Yuntao was also puzzled.

"The water in Xiaohei Stream is always calm; it definitely wouldn't make such a loud noise!"

"Could it be because of the rain two days ago?"

Zhu Tianlei nodded. What Zhang Yuntao said made some sense. As they were pondering this, they had already reached the side of Xiaohei Stream, only to find their path blocked by a huge rock that had fallen from the mountain.

"Come on, I'll carry you over!"

Zhu Tianlei beckoned with his finger, and Zhang Yuntao didn't refuse, throwing herself into Zhu Tianlei's arms. Zhu Tianlei bent down, kissed her lips lightly, then leaped forward to land beside Xiaohei Stream.

At this moment, the water level of Xiaohei Stream had risen significantly. What was originally a stream about one meter and forty-five centimeters wide now appeared to be more than three meters wide. Water flowed from the rocks, splashing up in all directions like a miniature waterfall.

No wonder the sound of running water was so loud.

Zhu Tianlei walked towards the water's edge and reached into it, feeling that the stream water was icy cold.

"Tianlei, look quick!"

Zhu Tianlei suddenly remembered the strands of pale blood that Qu Chuan had spat out, which were like thin white threads.

Yes, the water consumed by Hama Gou village and several surrounding villages came from the Daling River. In particular, for convenience in drinking water, many households in Hama Gou drew their water directly from the Xiaohei Stream.

The moment Zhu Tianlei figured this out, he felt a sudden clarity!

Now, he could be certain that these white bugs were the cause of the villagers' strange illnesses.

However, finding the cause was one thing; how to treat it remained unclear.

Zhang Yuntao stood to the side, seeing Zhu Tianlei frown and look deep in thought, so she knew not to disturb him. She quietly walked over to a nearby patch of grass, hoping to find some medicinal herbs.

"Tianlei, come quickly!"

While Zhu Tianlei was lost in his thoughts, he rushed over upon hearing Zhang Yuntao's call.

"Tianlei, look, these seem to be all medicinal herbs!"

Zhang Yuntao pointed at the endless sea of green before them.

At first, Zhu Tianlei was not impressed. But when he examined it closely, he opened his mouth in shock.

"Yuntao, you really are my lucky star!"

Zhu Tianlei excitedly picked up Zhang Yuntao and spun him around several times on the spot, making Zhang Yuntao dizzy.

"Yuntao, these are all herbs, and many of them are very rare. With these, there is hope for curing the villagers' illnesses!"
