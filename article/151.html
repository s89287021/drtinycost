Chapter 151: Chapter 151: Boisterous Capital

Máo Fang was quite urgent and got up without waiting for Wei Baoguo, heading straight for the floor where his lover's ward was located.

Zhu Tianlei, on the other hand, wasn't in a rush. He only started moving after everyone had left the conference room one by one.

Li Ping was walking at the back.

"Tianlei, this patient is of exceptional importance; be careful!"

Zhu Tianlei looked at Li Ping and knew that the latter's concern was genuine.

"Yes, thank you, I understand."

Zhu Tianlei and Li Ping had a somewhat friendly relationship after several interactions. The two walked side by side almost shoulder to shoulder.

Bai Song, who was walking ahead, turned his head slightly and glanced at them inadvertently. Although he quickly withdrew his gaze, Zhu Tianlei caught him in full view.

"Who is that person?"

Zhu Tianlei asked.

"Which one?"

Zhu Tianlei pointed with his finger, but just then, Bai Song turned the corner of the corridor and disappeared from sight.

"Oh, he's named Bai Song. We're college classmates, not a bad person, but too much of a thinker..."

Li Ping didn't want to talk about Bai Song. Although they were college classmates and colleagues at the same hospital, their relationship wasn't close.

Mainly because as soon as Bai Song graduated, he found a better position, metaphorically "marrying" himself into it. Despite lacking genuine talent or expertise, he climbed to the position of department chief with his wife's family background.

This person was quite arrogant and didn't get along well with most colleagues, but today's open targeting of someone was unprecedented for him.

"Oh, I see."

Zhu Tianlei didn't ask any further. He wasn't interested in Bai Song's condition; what intrigued him was the look Bai Song had given him just now.

It was far from friendly.

Soon, they arrived at the sterile ward.

"Is it you?"

A little nurse in a pink nursing gown smiled and tugged on Zhu Tianlei's sleeve.

What a coincidence! It was indeed the same little nurse who had been on duty outside the ICU last time.

"Yes, I'm back."

Thinking about his previous encounter, Zhu Tianlei couldn't help but chuckle to himself.

"It's wonderful. I thought I'd never see you again. The day we met, when I tried to talk to you, you just got into the elevator without even looking at me, and I..."

Zhu Tianlei glanced at Wei Baoguo and the others standing beside him, raising a finger to his lips in a shushing gesture.

The little nurse stuck out her tongue; she was simply too excited!

"Only three people are allowed in the sterile ward at a time. Director Mao, me, and he will go inside, so please wait outside!"

Wei Baoguo cast Zhu Tianlei a cold glance. Zhu Tianlei shrugged indifferently; Wei Baoguo had treated him with hostility from the start, and though Zhu didn't know why, it didn't matter to him.

Putting on his sterile gown, Zhu Tianlei nodded at Li Ping, whose eyes were filled with worry, then followed Wei Baoguo into the ward.

The sterile ward was even more advanced and rigorous than the ICU, as if the air itself had been highly purified by instruments.

Just as they entered the room, Mao Fang pounced towards the patient's bed.

"Old Qiao!"

Indeed, it was a married couple. From their current situation alone, one could see that their feelings for each other were still quite good.

"Mao Director, please calm down."

Wei Baoguo stood behind Mao Fang and said softly.

"Hurry over here. As long as you can cure Old Qiao, I’ll agree to anything you want."

Mao Fang ignored Wei Baoguo and looked at Zhu Tianlei behind him, speaking firmly.

To be honest, this Mao Fang was very domineering; her words were quite unpleasant to hear.

Although Zhu Tianlei understood the feeling of concern leading to chaos, he simply could not get close to this woman.

"I’ll do my best."

Zhu Tianlei swept past the two and walked to the bedside.

The man on the bed had a waxen complexion, sunken eyes, but there were dark spots around him that looked terrifying as if they had been injected with large amounts of mercury.

However, his cheeks were inexplicably swollen, almost obscuring his nose.

What he saw before his eyes was even more shocking than the photos he had seen on the projection.

Zhu Tianlei reached out to take the patient's pulse.

"Zhu Tianlei, this is a sterile ward. The patient’s condition is extremely weak; any external disturbance could worsen his illness. You’d better be careful."

"If the president is not reassured, then you can come yourself......"

"You......."

Wei Baoguo was at the brink of restraint. He was the president here; this was his domain, yet he had been repeatedly provoked by the other party.

"President Wei, don't get involved, let him handle it. If......"

Mao Fang only got halfway through her sentence, but Wei Baoguo understood perfectly. Yes, it wasn't a good job to take on. As someone who had practiced medicine for decades, he had never seen such a condition before. Zhu Tianlei was just an inexperienced rookie; even if his medical skills were high, he might not be able to cure the patient. If he failed, he would suffer the consequences!

Seeing that Wei Baoguo no longer spoke, Zhu Tianlei finally lowered his head again and placed his hand on the patient's wrist.

The pulse under his fingers seemed like a long snake writhing, but the way it moved was strange—it circled around, moving back and forth.

Zhu Tianlei took a very long time to diagnose the pulse.

Mao Fang's face remained grave throughout, while Wei Baoguo had an almost imperceptible hint of mockery on his face.

Hmph, the higher one climbs, the harder they fall. Let Zhu Tianlei be arrogant and repeatedly trample him underfoot; this time, he would make sure to let him know who can be provoked and who cannot.

A few more minutes passed before Zhu Tianlei finally withdrew his hand and turned around.

His expression was not a good one.

Mao Fang's heart sank with a thud.

Wei Baoguo subconsciously relaxed his clenched fist.

"I can cure him!"

What?

Wei Baoguo's eyes widened in disbelief as he tried to verify what Zhu Tianlei had said.

"Director Wei didn't hear wrong, I can cure him. In one hour, I will wake him up."

A recluse who cherishes fine phrases, his words are breathtaking!

Unexpectedly, this line of poetry popped into Wei Baoguo's mind.

"Really? Doctor Zhu, then start right away!"

Máo Fāng's eyes, once lifeless, suddenly sparked with a flame that quickly spread like wildfire, nearly causing her to grab Zhu Tianlei's shoulder in excitement.

"All right!"

Zhu Tianlei no longer spoke. To be honest, he was extremely confident, but it was also an attempt.

He reached into the inner pocket of his sterile gown and took out the 'Liu Zuan Wan' and silver needles he carried with him. This time, Wei Baoguo didn't say anything.

Zhu Tianlei placed the Liu Zuan Wan in the patient's mouth and then withdrew the silver needle.

The Lingyi's Seventeen Needles Technique.

Each needle penetrated 9.9mm into the subcutaneous tissue, precisely as such.

A flash of silver light appeared, the cold gleam resembling snow.
